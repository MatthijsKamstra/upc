Urban Paper Collective

Hello Fans and Followers,

The Urban Paper Collective is an international group of friends who share a passion for the design of papertoys of all sizes. We love to share our work and hang around online and in the real world. After several successful gallery shows, book releases and collaborations we decided to bundle our forces and establish the Urban Paper Collective. Our goal is to share our love for papertoys with the (digital) world, engage with our fans and make this website a portal to the paper worlds of each member in the collective. On this website you will find free papertoys created exclusively for this site, collaborations between papertoy artists, paper news, tips and much more.

To read more about each member of our collective, click on one of the names in the list at the right side of this page or select a name in the Who We Are drop-down menu at the top op this page.

If you’re interested in what we can do for you, check out our ‘What We Do‘ page to read more about the possibilities.

Hi there, and welcome to my Handcrafted Superior Felt Toys! I would like to invite you into my world of Felt, with inspiration coming from Japan/China, Papertoys, Anime, and cute, but hot stuffed animals. Next to the world of Felt, I also would like to show you some amazing nursery mobiles like you have never seen before. Products The toys and mobiles that I create are really amazing. They are made in high quality felt, or origami paper, and worked out into the little details. All is off course handcrafted, and made with lots of love and passion.
